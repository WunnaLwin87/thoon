About

Welcome to Thoon Thoon’s Final Fantasy XIV adventures. My name is Thoon Thoon, an avid player and enthusiast of the popular game Final Fantasy XIV. I have spent countless hours exploring the vast world of Eorzea, engaging with various types of content ranging from casual encounters to occasionally venturing into more challenging extreme battles. I created this site as a platform to share my experiences in the expansive world of Eorzea.

While I enjoy the thrill that savage content offers, my true passion lies in crafting and gathering. I take great pleasure in the meticulous process of creating and collecting valuable resources, uncovering the hidden intricacies of the game’s crafting system, and sharing my discoveries with fellow gamers.

However, this website isn’t just about me. It’s about helping you, the reader, navigate the intricacies of Final Fantasy XIV. Here, you will find my take on crafting, gathering, the gears I use, my crafting rotations, my raid and dungeon gear, and more. Whether you’re a beginner just setting foot in Eorzea or a seasoned player looking to optimize your crafting and gathering, there’s something here for you.

I believe in the importance of sharing my experiences – both the triumphs and the struggles – as I navigate through the game. My mission is to provide reliable, accessible, and practical information that will help you get the most out of your Final Fantasy XIV experience. I am committed to providing accurate and helpful information, but there may be times when I make a mistake or overlook something. I greatly appreciate and always welcome feedback from the community to help me correct and improve the content on this site.

So, join me as we explore the diverse activities that Final Fantasy XIV has to offer, all tailored to accommodate our personal playstyles and preferences. Let’s embark on this journey together!

Let’s explore Eorzea together!
